## 🌟 Overview
Vantigo is an open-source, all-in-one business management system designed to streamline your company's operations. Whether you’re a small startup or an established enterprise, Vantigo helps you manage customers, invoices, interactions, and products with ease. It's built to grow alongside your business, with a roadmap that includes features like automated invoicing, time tracking, project management, and much more!
